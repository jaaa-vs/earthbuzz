Lesson 1

What is SDG 13?

Let's start with the basics!

SDG stands for "Sustainable Development Goal"

The United Nations created 17 goals to make the world a better place by 2030. Goal number 13 is all about Climate Action!

Climate Action means taking care of Earth

Our planet is getting warmer because of pollution. Climate Action is about doing things to keep Earth cool and healthy for everyone.

üéØ The Main Goal:

"Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts"

In simple words: Let's work together to stop Earth from getting too hot!

Simple Ways to Get Started

Here are easy actions you can do TODAY to help the planet! üå±

1

Turn Off Lights

When you leave a room, turn off the lights! This saves electricity and helps reduce pollution.

üí° Pro Tip:

Use natural sunlight during the day instead of electric lights.

2

Use a Reusable Water Bottle

Instead of buying plastic bottles, get one reusable bottle you can refill every day!

üí° Pro Tip:

One reusable bottle can save hundreds of plastic bottles per year!

3

Recycle Your Trash

Separate paper, plastic, and glass from regular trash. This helps these materials get reused!

üí° Pro Tip:

Look for recycling bins with the ‚ôªÔ∏è symbol in your area.

4

Walk or Bike When Possible

For short trips, try walking or biking instead of taking a car. It's good for you AND the planet!

üí° Pro Tip:

Walking or biking also helps you stay healthy and fit!

5

Save Water

Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth and take shorter showers to save water.

üí° Pro Tip:

A 5-minute shower uses much less water than a bath!

6

Plant a Tree or Garden

Trees absorb CO2 (the bad gas that warms Earth). Planting one tree helps clean the air!

üí° Pro Tip:

Even a small pot plant on your windowsill helps!
